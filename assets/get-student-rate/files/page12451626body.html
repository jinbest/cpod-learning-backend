Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced? Nope, it's not as simple as that! At ChinesePod we understand that finding the right entry point for your Mandarin Chinese studies can be confusing. Learning a language, whether it be learning a few survival phrases or something you plan on building a lifelong relationship with, requires hard work and commitment. No one wants to be thrown in the deep end or be made to suffer through trite materials they have already mastered. Our content is aptly organised into seven levels with new lessons of trending topics published every week. With ChinesePod, not only you add Chinese language, you learn a new way to think, in Chinese!
Inside the pod, the system gives you multi-dimensional trainings in listening, reading, and speaking, and most importantly our training encourages you to discover and think in the Chinese language. Throughout, ChinesePod gives you a high degree of control and advanced website functionality to achieve the optimal immersive experience. The ChinesePod system is your next best option besides living in a Chinese-speaking envrionment.


Our lessons are situational, meaning you won't get stuck studying school subjects or sports when what you actually want to do is open a bank account or break up with someone. With over 4000 lessons, we have every situation that you could imagine covered and ready for you to study.


The ChinesePod system has helped hundreds of thousands of independent Chinese language learners. Following a placement test, students complete one hundred required study units, and a further fifty elective units in a designated level. Each level's grammar and vocabulary is correlated with day-to-day use and standardized exams in Chinese. After completing all required lessons, students either choose to take the level test, or expand their scope and sharpen their skills by engaging in different topics that are geared to their level.


At the end of the day, our main goal is to help you invest in yourself and reach your personal goals with Mandarin Chinese. Every week we publish new lessons so that you more you learn, you more you get to learn. We can't be the company we are without our loyal and awesome subscribers. Join the community of over one million people who have learned Mandarin Chinese with ChinesePod - we can't wait to meet you!
